About

AIML is primarily used in the development of chatbot systems and is particularly popular in the field of natural language processing and artificial intelligence. It allows developers to define patterns that match user inputs and generate appropriate responses based on those patterns.The language consists of categories, which are pairs of patterns and templates. The patterns define the user's input that the chatbot will recognize, and the templates define the corresponding response or output generated by the chatbot. AIML supports the use of wildcards, variables, and conditionals, allowing for more dynamic and personalized interactions.
