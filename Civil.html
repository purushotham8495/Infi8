About

Civil engineering is a branch of engineering that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure and the built environment. It involves the application of scientific and technical knowledge to plan, design, and oversee the construction of structures such as buildings, bridges, roads, dams, airports, and water supply systems.Civil engineering requires strong analytical and problem-solving skills, as well as an understanding of engineering principles, codes, and regulations. It is a field that contributes to the development and improvement of the built environment, enhancing the quality of life for communities and societies.

  • Geotechnical engineering
  • Construction engineering
  • Hydraulic engineering
