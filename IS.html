About

Information Science is a multidisciplinary field that focuses on the study, organization, analysis, and management of information. It explores how information is created, stored, retrieved, and utilized in various contexts, including organizations, industries, and society as a whole. Information Science combines aspects of computer science, library science, cognitive science, social sciences, and management to understand and address the complexities of information in the digital age.
